phahita sandwish

  • Time:10min
  • Serves: sandwish
Ingredients Quantity
cheese 100g
meat 150g
corn 30g

to prepare: We boil the meat, then put the corn on top and at the end we add the cheese.

lentil soup

  • Time:45min
  • Serves: soup plate
Ingredients Quantity
lentil 100g
onions 150g
salt and black pepper 30g

to prepare:Put the lentils and onions in a cooking pot, then boil the lentils until they are soft for 45 minutes, then add salt and pepper.

spinash in the oven

  • Time:30min
  • Serves: plate
Ingredients Quantity
spinash 300g
onions 150g
salt and black pepper 30g
tomato 100g
cheese 50g

to prepare:We cook the spinach with onions and tomatoes until they are soft and then put them in a clay pot and put some cheese on top and put them in the oven for 15 minutes until they are browned and take out.
